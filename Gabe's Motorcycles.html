Gabe's Motorcycles

Gabe's vintage motorcycle collection.

In all seriousness, motorcycles and bikes are a rather large part of my life. I spend a lot of time in my garage back home building, restoring, and selling vintage motorcycles. I have a particular liking to the classic enduro types of bikes. I have even brought one of my motorcycles to ride around campus. Unfortunately, I often have to sell many bikes to make room for more. But this has given me the oppurtunity to own a lot of cool bikes. One day in the future, I hope to own my own business where I can use my skills and passion of motorcycles.

A list of some classic motorcycles Gabe has owned.

  1. 1977 Kawaskai Ke200
  2. 1978 Kawasaki Ke125
  3. 1978 Kawasaki Ke100 G4TR
  4. 1973 Yamaha DT175
  5. 1974 Yamaha DT175
  6. 1992 Yamaha XT225
  7. 1979 Honda XL185
